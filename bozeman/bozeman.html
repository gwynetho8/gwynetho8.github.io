About Bozeman Montana

Bozeman was founded in 1864 by John Bozeman. Bozeman is located in southwestern Montana. It is also known as the Gallatin County. It is home to world-class fly fishing, hunting, golfing, biking, kayaking, climbing, snowboarding, rafting, skiing, hiking, camping, and other recreational opportunities. Bozeman has a population of around 46,596 people.
